Carrie Hott

The apple music app is one of my most frequented. Whether it is queuing music, making a playlist, or just listening to music, I interact with this app in several locations on my phone and very often.

Part of my morning routine involves checking the weather app. I’ll often check the weather in San Francisco as well as wherever in the world my friends and family are, just to feel a little more connected.

A little bit about the music I listen The bus is my primary mode of transportation and Apple Maps help me ensure that I get where I’m going on time. I use it most often to check the bus times, especially taking the 33 bus to school.

I am always taking photos or screenshots and I love looking back through my camera roll. For the most part my photos don’t end up anywhere, but I enjoy going back every now and then recall old memories.

Since moving to San Francisco for college, most of my friends and family live out of state. I love being able to check on everyone and see where all m y people are.

I check Gmail multiple times a day, even though it’s mostly spam. Sometimes it’s important, oftentimes it’s not, but I’ll still check every time I’m notified of a new email.

Canvas helps me stay up to date with my school work and make sure that everything that needs to be done is accounted for. I don’t check it as often as I used to, but it definitely has a permanent space on my home page.

I have almost fifty preset alarms in my clock app that depending on the day I select the ones I need for the next day. In addition to my alarm clock, I also use it to set timers and check the world clock for my friends who live abroad.

Zillow is my most recent addiction app. I find myself checking it when I’m bored, almost like social media.

I look things up constantly. My most recent and frequent searches include recipes, NYTs Games, random facts that I need to check, and more.

Instagram is one of those apps that I am so conditioned to use that I can’ imagine getting rid of it. It’s honestly how I get most of my news, friend and family updates, and entertainment.

If I’m not taking the bus or walking, then I’m probably in a Waymo. I take Waymo all over the city, but especially to work or to my sister’s apartment in Russian Hill.
